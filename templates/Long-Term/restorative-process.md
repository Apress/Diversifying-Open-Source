---

## When restorative approaches apply

Restorative processes may be used when:

- participants consent
- safety can be maintained
- repair is possible

They are not used when immediate protective action is required.

---

## Process outline

1. Intake and acknowledgment
2. Assessment of impact and risk
3. Agreement on restorative steps
4. Follow-up and documentation

---

## Possible outcomes

- acknowledgment of harm
- commitments to change
- boundary-setting
- mediated conversation
- referral to other processes

---

## Documentation

Outcomes are documented with care for privacy.
Anonymized learnings may be shared when appropriate.
