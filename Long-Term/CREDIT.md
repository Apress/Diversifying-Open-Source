Credit here functions as **institutional memory**.

---

## What is credited

Credit may include:

- code and architecture
- documentation and review
- accessibility and localization
- moderation and conflict work
- governance and facilitation
- funding and operational labor

---

## How credit is assigned

Credit draws from:

- contribution taxonomy labels
- changelog entries
- decision records

Requests for correction or addition are welcome.
